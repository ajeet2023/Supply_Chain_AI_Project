# LLM Reporting Sample Email

This sample shows the executive email draft generated by `src/llm_reporting.py` using the prompt and metrics defined in the script.

## Sample Executive Email Draft

Subject: Supply Chain Delivery Performance and Recommendation

Hello,

We have completed the initial deployment of the new predictive delivery model and data pipeline. Today’s run shows that the model is accurately identifying late deliveries, with a predictive accuracy of 91.94%. At the same time, we observed that the packages currently in “late” status represent $150,000 in revenue and are experiencing an average delay of 2.5 days.

This insight confirms that the model is effectively isolating high-risk shipments before they leave the warehouse. By prioritizing these orders through the routing team, we can reduce customer delays and minimize the financial impact of late deliveries.

I recommend that the warehouse routing team immediately begin using the AI-generated risk scores to prioritize high-risk packages. This will help improve on-time delivery rates while protecting revenue and customer satisfaction.
